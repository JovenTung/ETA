Who we are

A club that nurtures talent and friendships.

High-quality coaching for every age and level, in a welcoming club.

Our mission: develop confident, skilled players through expert coaching, while keeping it enjoyable. Our vision: to be a cornerstone of the local tennis community.

From a first lesson to competition and adult social play, we help every player take the next step.

"We don't just teach tennis. We build a community united by a love of the game, where talent grows and friendships last."

Lynton Joseph, Founder & Operations Manager

Our venues

East Coburg.

Our home is East Coburg Tennis Club, 45 Pentridge Blvd, Coburg VIC 3058, open seven days, 6am to 10pm.

  • Child safety

    Zero-tolerance towards child abuse. All coaches hold Working With Children Checks, follow our code of conduct and complete regular child-safety training.

  • Growth & wellbeing

    Coaching that develops players on the court and good habits off it.

  • Policies & support

    Clear terms, fair policies and a team that is easy to reach if you need help.

  • In the community

    We support local causes including Moreland Relay for Life, FIDA football, the Federation Cup Foundation and Sands.
