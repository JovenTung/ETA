Workshops

Sharpen a specific part of your game.

Focused small-group sessions that go deep on one area. Full-day and half-day options.

  • Doubles workshop

    Positioning, point construction, court communication and net play, so you and your partner click.

  • Technique & strategy

    Interactive drills that build skills and tactical understanding for match play.

  • Flexible scheduling

    Full-day and half-day options across school terms and holidays.
