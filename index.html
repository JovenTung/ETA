Who we are

A local club built around coaching and community.

A Coburg club built on a simple belief: good coaching and community make better players. Every age and level, from first lesson to competitive squad.

  • Accredited coaches

    12 qualified AATC coaches with real competitive experience.

  • Tailored training

    Programs matched to your level and goals.

  • Community first

    Social events, leagues and family days that feel like home.
