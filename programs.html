Official Cardio Tennis program Cardio Tennis

Adult Social Cardio Tennis.

A fast, fun, 45-minute fitness class run to music and grouped by level. Pick the pace that suits you, from a steady starter session to a full-on workout.

Cardio Lite

A lower-impact cardio tennis session designed for players who want to move, hit plenty of balls and enjoy a fun workout at a steadier pace. Great for beginners, returning players or anyone wanting a lighter session.

Cardio Play

A fun, social session combining tennis-based games, movement and point play. Ideal for players who want a mix of fitness, hitting and match-style activities without the pressure of formal competition.

Cardio Classic

Our regular cardio tennis workout with plenty of hitting, movement, music and energy. A great all-round session for players who want to improve their fitness while enjoying a fast-paced tennis workout.

Cardio Max

Our highest-energy cardio tennis session for those who want to be pushed. Expect faster drills, more movement, higher intensity and a serious workout, with plenty of fun along the way. This would not suit beginner players.

Ready to give it a go?

Join the tennis-inspired workout that combines music, fun and fitness. Grouped by level, no experience needed and racquets provided.
