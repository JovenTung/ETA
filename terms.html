By accessing and using the Elite Tennis Academy website and services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood and agree to be bound by these terms and conditions.

Missed lessons and failure to attend

Our coaches rely on your coaching commitment and need notice before a scheduled lesson.

Private lessons

For evening sessions, please notify your coach before 10am on the day of the session to be entitled to a make-up. For morning sessions, please notify your coach by 7pm the night before to be entitled to a make-up.

Group lessons

If you cannot attend your group class, it is your responsibility to cancel it through our online portal. If at least 4 hours' notice is given, an automatic lesson credit is applied to your account. Credits can be used at any time to make up a cancelled class.

Make-up and cancellation policy

For long-term injury or illness, please liaise with the Head Coach to reschedule missed lessons and guarantee your place. A credit may be applied in these situations, and a medical certificate is required.

Lessons missed through a pupil's absence can be made up at the end of term, in the first week of the school holidays. A medical certificate is required.

Wet weather and extreme heat

We run lessons as scheduled, so please do not assume a lesson is not going ahead. Lessons are cancelled if the temperature is over 36 degrees Celsius, or if the courts are unplayable due to excess water. We will send an SMS or email notification when possible. Credits are applied for any wash-outs or heat-outs that cannot be made up during the term.
